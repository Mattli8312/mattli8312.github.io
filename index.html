Who Am I?
Intro:
Hi! I'm Matthew Lian. I was born and raised in New York and moved to the greater Chicago areas when I was 10. I graduated from the University of Illionis at Urbana Champaign after earning my Bachelors in Computer Engineering.
Past Experience:
I previously worked as a full stack developer at Epic Systems after graduation on Epic's surgery suite: OpTime. I had the opportunity of working with a lot of talented developers and design new features enhancing workflow experiences for perioperative nurses. I primarily worked with typescript and React, developing features using the .NET framework.
Current Experience:
I currently work as a software engineer in Ithaca NY for a civil engineering solutions firm known as Vector Magnetics and am still very much a newb in that industry, but am incredibly excited to work with the folks I had the opportunity to talk with at that company.
Tech stacks and programming languages:
I have professional experience with the following web development languages and frameworks:
  • Typescript
  • React

  • .NET ASP and Entity Framework
  • SQL
I also have knowledge with the following tech as well:
